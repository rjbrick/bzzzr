What do these numbers mean?
Heat score
The big number on the right. A single reading, usually 0 to about 3, of how unusual and how positive a stock's Reddit attention is right now. It rewards buy-side talk and options/gamma-squeeze chatter, and dampens names where the tone is bearish or short-side. Most sit below 1.0; above roughly 1.7 is rare.
🔥 Hot
Strong, bullish surge, confirmed by real trading volume.
🌡️ Heating
Attention climbing fast, but volume or tone has not fully confirmed it.
👀 Watch
Mild or early interest. On the radar, not breaking out.
⚡ options
Heavy options and gamma-squeeze discussion, the kind of setup WSB piles into.
Why line
A plain-English summary of what is actually driving the name right now (surge, buying tone, options chatter, volume).
1D / 5D / 1M / YTD
The stock's price change over 1 day, 5 days, 1 month, and year-to-date. Green is up, red is down.
mentions
Posts and comments naming the stock across the tracked subreddits in the latest window.
2.4× vol
Today's share volume versus the stock's recent average.

Buzzzr measures attention, not value. A high score means people are talking and leaning bullish, not that a stock is a good buy. Trending names are often late and frequently fall. Do your own research.
